Fuse testing quick notes:
- Set the multimeter to DC volts.
- Use a known good ground reference.
- Test both tabs of the fuse.
- 12V on both sides usually means the fuse element is intact and both sides share the same potential.
- 12V on one side and 0V on the other often suggests a blown/open fuse.
- 0V on both sides may mean no upstream supply or a measurement reference issue.
